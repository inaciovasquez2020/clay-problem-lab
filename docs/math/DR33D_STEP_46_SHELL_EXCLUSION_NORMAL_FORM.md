# DR33D Step 46 — Shell-Exclusion Normal Form

## Status
Conditional.

## Normal form

The shell-exclusion theorem is in normal form when stated as
\[
\mathcal P(u)\Longrightarrow
\Bigl(
E_{j+1}(u)\le E_j(u),\
E_j(u)=0\ (j<j_0),\
\mathfrak C(E(u))\le 1-\delta,\
S(E(u))\ge m
\Bigr).
\]

No weaker normal form is currently known to eliminate both
one-shell vanishing concentration and block-monotone vanishing concentration.

## Lock sentence

Step 46 records the normal form of the terminal shell-exclusion theorem.
