## Minimal Missing Lemma

For every terminal-admissible generator chi,

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
\forall \zeta\in W_{\mathrm{term}}^\perp,\quad
\langle \chi,\zeta\rangle_{L^2}=0.
\]

## Consequence

If this lemma is proved, then

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
(I-\Pi_W)\chi=0
\Longrightarrow
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=0.
\]

Thus the remaining theorem-level frontier reduces to deriving weak terminal orthogonality from the actual RA1n terminal grammar.
