# RA1n Terminal Residual Vanishing from Admissibility Theorem

Status: CONDITIONAL STRUCTURAL THEOREM

## Target

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
\|r_W(\chi)\|_2=0.
\]

## Terminal Residual Functional

Define

\[
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi):=\|r_W(\chi)\|_2^2.
\]

## Admissibility Saturation Hypothesis

Assume terminal admissibility is exactly residual saturation:

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=0.
\]

## Theorem

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
\|r_W(\chi)\|_2=0.
\]

## Proof

Assume

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi).
\]

By admissibility saturation,

\[
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=0.
\]

By definition,

\[
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=\|r_W(\chi)\|_2^2.
\]

Thus

\[
\|r_W(\chi)\|_2^2=0.
\]

Since norms are nonnegative,

\[
\|r_W(\chi)\|_2=0.
\]

## Consequence

By `RA1N_WEAK_ORTHOGONALITY_FROM_RESIDUAL_NORM_THEOREM.md`,

\[
\|r_W(\chi)\|_2=0
\Longrightarrow
\mathcal W_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=0.
\]

## Remaining Non-Conditional Obligation

Prove the residual saturation identity from the actual admissibility rule:

\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)
\Longrightarrow
\mathcal R_{\mathrm{term}}(\chi)=0.
\]
