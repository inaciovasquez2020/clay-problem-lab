## Canonical remainder

\[
r_k(\xi)=\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi).
\]

## Weakest sufficient closure lemma

\[
\forall \alpha\in\mathbb N^3\ \exists C_\alpha<\infty\ \forall k\ge 0,\ \forall |\xi|\sim 2^k,
\qquad
\left|
\partial_\xi^\alpha\!\bigl(\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi)\bigr)
\right|
\le
C_\alpha\,2^{-k(2+|\alpha|)}.
\]

## Equivalent weighted form

\[
\sup_{|\xi|\sim 2^k}
2^{k(1+|\alpha|)}
\left|
\partial_\xi^\alpha\!\bigl(\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi)\bigr)
\right|
\le
C_\alpha\,2^{-k}.
\]

## Consequence

\[
\sum_{k\ge 0}
\sup_{|\xi|\sim 2^k}
2^{k(1+|\alpha|)}
\left|
\partial_\xi^\alpha\!\bigl(\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi)\bigr)
\right|
<\infty
\Longrightarrow
\text{unconditional RA1n closure}.
\]
