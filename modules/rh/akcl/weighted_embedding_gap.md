# Weighted Embedding Gap for AKCL

## Minimal Missing Lemma

For f in admissible Mellin-bandlimited class:

\[
\operatorname{supp} M_f(\tfrac12+it)\subset[-B,B],
\]

there exists C_B > 0 such that

\[
\int_0^\infty |f(x)|^2 dx
\le
C_B \int_0^\infty |f(x)|^2 e^{-x} dx.
\]
