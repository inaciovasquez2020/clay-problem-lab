# DR33D Step 31 — Extra Invariant Candidate for Linear Upgrade

## Candidate invariant

Let
\[
S:=\sum_{j\in\mathbb Z}E_j^{1+\theta}.
\]
Introduce the size floor
\[
\mathfrak J(E):=\mathbf 1_{\{S\ge m\}}
\]
for some fixed \(m>0\), equivalently the side condition
\[
S\ge m.
\]

## Upgrade mechanism

Under
\[
S\ge m>0,
\]
one has
\[
S^{1/(1+\theta)}
\le
m^{-\theta/(1+\theta)}S.
\]

Combined with Step 28,
\[
A^2\le C(\theta,j_0)\,S^{1/(1+\theta)}
\]
yields
\[
A^2\le C(\theta,j_0)m^{-\theta/(1+\theta)}S.
\]

## Logical role

This is the minimal size-control invariant that upgrades the Step 28 sublinear power to linear control.

CANDIDATE EXTRA INVARIANT — power-gap removal works once a positive lower size floor is imposed.
