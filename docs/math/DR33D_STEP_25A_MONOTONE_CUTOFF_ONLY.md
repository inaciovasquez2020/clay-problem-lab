# DR33D Step 25A — Monotone+Cutoff Only

## Statement

Let \(0<\theta<1\). Ask whether there exists \(C=C(\theta,j_0)<\infty\) such that every nonnegative sequence \((E_j)\) satisfying
\[
E_{j+1}\le E_j,
\qquad
E_j=0\ \text{for }j<j_0
\]
obeys
\[
\left(\sum_{j\in\mathbb Z}2^{-j}E_j^{1/2}\right)^2
\le
C\sum_{j\in\mathbb Z}E_j^{1+\theta}.
\]

## Status

FALSE — disproved by the block-monotone family of Step 30.
