# RA1n Transfer Decomposition Lemma

Status: OPEN.

## Target Lemma

There exist certified quantities \(M_k\) and \(E_k\) such that

\[
\mathcal T_{\mathrm{RA1n}}(k)=M_k-E_k,
\]

with

\[
M_k\ge
\mathrm{Curv}_k\mathrm{Gap}_k|\partial_\xi\widehat G_k|
\]

and

\[
E_k\le
\|P_k\widehat G_k\|_{\mathrm{err}}.
\]

## Consequence

\[
\mathcal T_{\mathrm{RA1n}}(k)
\ge
\mathrm{Curv}_k\mathrm{Gap}_k|\partial_\xi\widehat G_k|
-
\|P_k\widehat G_k\|_{\mathrm{err}}.
\]

## Certified Conditional Values

\[
\mathrm{Curv}_k=2,\qquad
\mathrm{Gap}_k=1,\qquad
|\partial_\xi\widehat G_k|=1,\qquad
\|P_k\widehat G_k\|_{\mathrm{err}}=0.
\]

Therefore, if this lemma is proved,

\[
\mathcal T_{\mathrm{RA1n}}(k)\ge 2>0.
\]

## Weakest Missing Object

A definition-level identity identifying the RA1n transfer quantity with one positive curvature-gap term minus one projection-error term.
