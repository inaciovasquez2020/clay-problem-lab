# DDYO RA1n Direct Symbol-Derivative Frontier

## Canonical remainder

\[
r_k(\xi):=\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi).
\]

## Exact remaining theorem

Prove that for every multi-index \(\alpha\in\mathbb N^3\),
\[
\sup_{k\ge 0}\sup_{|\xi|\sim 2^k}
2^{k(1+|\alpha|)}
\left|
\partial_\xi^\alpha\!\bigl(\widehat G_k(\xi)-P_k\widehat G_k(\xi)\bigr)
\right|
<\infty.
\]

## Admissibility constraints

The proof must be derived directly from the canonical formula
\[
r_k=\widehat G_k-P_k\widehat G_k,
\]
without replacing the argument by an inadmissible volume-absorption step.

## Consequence

This theorem is the remaining direct-symbol frontier between the extracted canonical
definition of \(r_k\) and unconditional RA1n closure.
