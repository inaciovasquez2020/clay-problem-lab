# AKCL Mass Defect

## Observation

Even after normalization by D(L):

E_norm[f] / ||f||^2 → 0 as L → ∞

## Minimal Missing Ingredient

Mass escape:

∃ regions R_L such that
∑_{x∈R_L} w(x) → 0
but contribution to E[f] negligible

## Single Structural Fix

Introduce localized coercivity:

E[f] ≥ c0 ∑_{x∈Core(L)} |f(x)|^2 w(x)

where Core(L) excludes escaping tails

## Equivalent Form

Global coercivity ⇔ no mass escape

## New Invariant

Tail mass:

T(L) = ∑_{x large} w(x)

Control T(L) ⇒ restore coercivity
