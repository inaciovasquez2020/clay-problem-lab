# AKCL Uniformization Step

## Defect

c(L) → 0 as L → ∞

## Single Structural Fix

Introduce normalized energy:

E_norm[f] = (1 / D(L)) E[f]

where

D(L) = sup_x ∑_y K(x,y) w(y)

## Target Inequality

E_norm[f] ≥ c0 ||f||^2

with c0 > 0 independent of L

## Reduction

It suffices to prove:

E[f] ≥ c0 D(L) ||f||^2

## New Invariant

D(L) captures kernel mass growth.

Uniform coercivity ⇔ controlled growth of D(L).
