## Candidate paired estimate

For \(|j-k|\le C\),
\[
\left|
\int \mathcal H'_\tau(\omega_j)\cdot[\widetilde P_j,S_k\!\cdot\nabla]\omega_k\,dx
\right|
\le
C\,2^{-j}2^{-k}\,
\|\nabla S_k\|_{\mathrm{BMO}}\,
\|\omega_k\|_{H^1},
\]
or an equivalent shell-localized \(H^1\)-BMO paracommutator bound implying
\[
|\mathsf R_{j,k}|
\le
C\,2^{-j}2^{-k}\,
\|\nabla S_k\|_{L^\infty}\,
\|\omega_k\|_{L^1}.
\]
