# RA1n Transverse Rayleigh-Gap Certificate Theorem

Status: CONDITIONAL STRUCTURAL THEOREM

## Target

Prove

\[
A_{\perp}\ge \eta I
\qquad
\text{on}
\qquad
V_{\mathrm{RA1n}}^\perp .
\]

## Finite Transverse Basis Hypothesis

Assume the admissible transverse terminal space is generated by a finite orthonormal family

\[
W_{\perp}
=
\operatorname{span}\{\psi_1,\dots,\psi_m\}
\subset
V_{\mathrm{RA1n}}^\perp .
\]

Assume every transverse terminal residual satisfies

\[
r\in W_{\perp}.
\]

## Matrix Certificate

Define

\[
M_{ij}:=\langle \psi_i,A_{\perp}\psi_j\rangle_{L^2}.
\]

Assume \(M\) is Hermitian and satisfies the finite Rayleigh lower bound

\[
c^*Mc\ge \eta\,c^*c
\qquad
\forall c\in\mathbb C^m
\]

for some

\[
\eta>0.
\]

## Theorem

For every

\[
r\in W_{\perp},
\]

one has

\[
\langle r,A_{\perp}r\rangle_{L^2}
\ge
\eta\|r\|_2^2.
\]

## Proof

Let

\[
r=\sum_{j=1}^m c_j\psi_j.
\]

Since \(\{\psi_j\}_{j=1}^m\) is orthonormal,

\[
\|r\|_2^2=c^*c.
\]

Also,

\[
\langle r,A_{\perp}r\rangle_{L^2}
=
c^*Mc.
\]

By the finite Rayleigh lower bound,

\[
c^*Mc\ge \eta\,c^*c.
\]

Therefore

\[
\langle r,A_{\perp}r\rangle_{L^2}
\ge
\eta\|r\|_2^2.
\]

Thus

\[
A_{\perp}\ge \eta I
\qquad
\text{on}
\qquad
W_{\perp}.
\]

## Consequence

By `RA1N_TRANSVERSE_SPECTRAL_GAP_COERCIVITY_THEOREM.md`,

\[
A_{\perp}\ge \eta I
\Longrightarrow
E_{\perp}(r)\ge \eta\|r\|_2^2.
\]

By `RA1N_NORMALIZED_TRANSVERSE_COERCIVITY_THEOREM.md`,

\[
E_{\perp}(r)\ge \eta\|r\|_2^2
\Longrightarrow
E_{\mathrm{term}}(\psi)\ge E_*+\eta
\]

for normalized transverse terminal packets.

By `RA1N_TRANSVERSE_ENERGY_GAP_EXCLUSION_THEOREM.md`, this excludes nonzero transverse terminal generators.

Prove that every admissible transverse terminal residual lies in the certified finite transverse space:

r=(I-\Pi_V)F,
\qquad
F\in\mathcal P_{\mathrm{RA1n}}^{\mathrm{terminal}}
\Longrightarrow

\]
