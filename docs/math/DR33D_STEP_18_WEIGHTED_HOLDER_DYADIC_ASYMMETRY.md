# DR33D Step 18 — Weighted Hölder Inequality with Dyadic Decay Asymmetry

## Status
CONDITIONAL

The weighted quantity is
\[
\sum_j 2^{-j} E_j^{1/2}.
\]

Assume
\[
S:=\sum_{j\in\mathbb Z} E_j^{1+\theta}\le M<\infty
\]
and
\[
E_j=0 \quad \text{for } j<j_0.
\]

Let
\[
q:=2(1+\theta)
\]
and
\[
p:=\frac{q}{q-1}=\frac{2(1+\theta)}{1+2\theta}.
\]

The resulting constant is
\[
C(j_0,\theta,M).
\]

CONDITIONAL ROUTE — closes Step 16 only under the added boundedness and support assumptions.
